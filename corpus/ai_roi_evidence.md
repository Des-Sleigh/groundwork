# ROI Evidence for AI Demand Forecasting

> Illustrative fixture corpus for Groundwork's offline demo. Example values only.

Reported returns from AI demand forecasting in logistics cluster around three
levers: lower inventory carrying cost, fewer stockouts, and better asset
utilization. Case write-ups commonly cite inventory reductions in the range of
10–20% and forecast-error improvements of 20–30% over prior baselines.

ROI evidence quality varies widely. Vendor case studies tend to report best-case
single-site results, while independent studies emphasize that gains depend on
baseline maturity: firms with weak existing forecasting see the largest jump,
and firms already using strong statistical methods see smaller incremental gains.

Payback periods of 9–18 months are frequently described once data integration is
complete, with the largest costs front-loaded into the first deployment.
